Mindful Escape
Music Album produced by Cetaceous
Featuring: Elliot Platt, Ashesndreams, Jefke, Chewba, Yoda, Sgaja, ODARKA
Abstract

This album is about the stress, pressure, and fear we feel in daily life — and the places in our minds where we go to feel free and relaxed. It has 9 songs plus a special bonus track called Lagoon (featuring Galaxy Mix). Seven different artists worked together, using singing, rapping, and spoken word to share their thoughts. The music shows the struggle between life's challenges and finding peace. This album is a creative way to explore how we can stay balanced in a busy world.
