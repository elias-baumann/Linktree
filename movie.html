directed by Elias Baumann alias Cetaceous
Abstract

The Mental Escape is an introspective audiovisual documentary about finding balance in today’s fast-paced world. It explores the stress of work, big-city life, and daily routines, contrasted with the human yearning for mental liberation. Through evocative visuals and a self-produced music album, the film uses rap, spoken word, and melodies to reflect on the constant search for happiness and how quickly it can fade. Mental Escape invites viewers to think about the balance between the preasure of daily life and the chase for inner peace.
