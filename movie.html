directed by Elias Baumann alias Cetaceous
Abstract

Mental Escape is a documentary about finding balance in a busy world. It shows the stress of work, city life, and daily routines, but also the deep need for mental freedom. With powerful images and music — rap, spoken word, and melodies — the film explores how people search for happiness and how quickly it can disappear. Mental Escape makes you think about the struggle between everyday stress and the desire for inner peace.
